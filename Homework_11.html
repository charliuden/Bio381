My Data:

For todays assignment I will be using data on life stage transitions of centaurea (knapweed). Each row is a population of centaurea. Eventually, I will use this data to build population matrices, but right now I am just going to use ggplot to peek at the data.

Variables: g = germination rate (September - July) ss = seed survival (s-s) (Sept - July) ssj = survival of newly-germinated seedlings within first year (s-j) sjv = survival (maturation) of established seedlings to vegetative juveniles (j-v) sv = survival of vegetative juveniles (v-v) sf = survival of adult flowering plants (f-f) mjf = maturation, seedlings to flowering plants (j-f); probably unlikely mvf = maturation, vegetative juveniles to flowering plants (v-f) f = per capita fecundity (viable seeds)n

C <- read.table("CentaureaVitalRates.csv",header=TRUE,sep=",", stringsAsFactors=FALSE)
#take out NA's 
cleanC <- C[complete.cases(C[1:10]),]

             aes(x=Species, y=sf, 
                 fill= I("greenyellow"))) +
  labs(title="Adult survival rate of two species of knapweed", 
       y="Survival rate of flowering adults", 
       x="Species") +
  geom_boxplot()
